We are a social enterprise aiming to address the proliferation of adulterated honey.

Overview. Naturally farmed honey and bee products take long time to harvest so some bee farmers perform the unethical practice of feeding their bees with sugar syrup, instead of natural nectar. Bees do not convert sugar syrup into another product. What they are fed is what they produce.

Its Effect to Health. Just in the Philippines, there are more than 3.7 million patients diagnosed with diabetes. Several studies have found eating authentic honey may increase insulin levels and decrease blood sugar. In effect, some of the diabetic patients look for healthier sweetener alternative in the form of honey, without knowing that the quality of their purchase is unsafe for their consumption. There is few awareness that honey farmed from bees fed with sugar syrup do contain high sugar levels.
