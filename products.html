Products

Honey

A sweet sticky material made by bees from the nectar of flowers and stored in a honeycomb for food. High-Quality Honey Is rich in Antioxidants. According to new research, if you can switch out your intake of refined sugar and use pure raw honey instead, the health benefits can be enormous (Source). Honey is a great brain booster, lifting brain fatigue, and improving alertness.

Royal Jelly

Royal jelly is used for asthma, hay fever, liver disease, pancreatitis, type 2 diabetes, diabetic foot ulcers, sleep troubles (insomnia), and fatigue (tiredness) secondary to cancer.

Bee Pollen

A ball or pellet of field-gathered flower pollen packed by worker honeybees, and used as the primary food source for the hive. Bee pollen is harvested as food for humans, with various health claims. It is a complete protein, rich in vitamins, minerals, enzymes, amino acids and anti-oxidants, it is considered an immune system builder that will also enhance vitality.


Propolis

Propolis and its extracts have numerous applications in treating various diseases due to its anti-inflammatory, antioxidant, antibacterial, and anticancer properties.

Beeswax

The wax secreted by bees to make honeycombs and used to make wood polishes and candles.
